Automerge-RS

Automerge is a document CRDT designed to track and merge changes from different clients with a minimum of disruption. The Automerge-RS project aims to reimplement Automerge in Rust with the dual goals of improving performance and also bringing Automerge to more platforms. Our hope is that the Automerge-RS backend will become the definitive Automerge implementation and that simple language and environment specific front-ends will proliferate across all platforms, ensuring consistent behaviour in all environments.
