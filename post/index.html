Towards Production: Getting in Shape

Behind Automerge-RS In 2017, Ink & Switch decided to collaborate with Martin Kleppmann to see if we could build an easy to use CRDT in javascript based on Martin’s research. By and large, I think we were successful, which can be seen in Automerge v0.14.1. While we hit the goals of being a correct CRDT that was easy to use, there were a number of shortcomings. It was too slow, and as every change in the history was stored completely in an uncompressed format, the footprint of a document was too large.
